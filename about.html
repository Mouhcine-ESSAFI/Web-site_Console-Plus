ABOUT CONSOLE PLUS

Welcome to 'Console Gamer Magazine', a specialized publication focusing on one retro games console at a time making each issue a must have for serious and casual gamers alike.


Created for gamers, by gamers, our magazine takes a look at the history, technology, games, accessories and cultural impact that made these consoles an essential part of our generation. Each issue is written and designed to deliver maximum entertainment for the reader by providing in-depth research, creative visual design & endless pages of content.


We've just begun our adventure as a fully independant digital magazine. Help us on our journey by subscribing and in return we'll send you a copy of the brand new 'Console Gamer Magazine' each time it's released.

thank you for the support!"
